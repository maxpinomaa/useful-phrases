How to use (and how not to use) this website

For more or less serious students of Finnish, this website does not pretend to be a comprehensive language course. There are many ways to get fluent in a language, but usually you need a combination of different methods and materials. I will not, for example, teach much grammar here. There are many books and websites for that.

This website focuses more on conversation, the amazing world of human interaction. Certain expressions are very useful and fun in that world.

Links for free high quality websites that teach Finnish

Surface languages has a nice collection of sentences in many languages, even in Finnish. This site has useful words and many sentences (with audio!) for different situations. And everything is free, including the mobile apps.

Even if you only use the free version, LingQ is a great website for learning languages. It has material for Finnish too. Lingq has lots of texts with audio. Some are long. Some are short. There is also a mobile app for LingQ.
