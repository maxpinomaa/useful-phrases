Quick learning tips

Learning languages is much easier when you follow some of the advice of experienced polyglots. It is inspiring to see interviews of people who speak 10+ languages fluently (even while having a life!). If they can do ten or more, you can easily do at least one more, right?

Experienced polyglots have found ways of making the process more fun and interesting. From my own experience I can say that life "standing on the shoulders of giants" is much more fun than before. More about the bigwig polyglots.

Are there shortcuts to fluency? Learning a language is usually a long road, unless you are learning a language that is very similar to one that you already know. However, in my opinion, learning conversational connectors and common questions can be seen as some sort of shortcuts.

When are you ready to start speaking? There is no clear right or wrong answer to this. Personally I tend to start speaking very early on by learning to deal with the common questions.
