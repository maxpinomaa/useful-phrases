Links to other great websites

I hope some of these websites will be useful and enjoyable for you. I have used all of these in my own language studies.

Links for learning Spanish

Bookbox youtube channel
These are stories with Spanish audio and Spanish subtitles. Although they are meant for children, some of the stories are quite interesting. Anyway it's nice change for more serious language learning material. Free! Level: beginner and intermediate

Surface Languages - Spanish
A nice collection of sentences for different situations. Includes high quality audio too. Free! Level: beginner and intermediate

Yabla Spanish
This is a great collection of videos for learning Spanish. There are videos for many levels. And many of the videos are TV originally meant for native speakers. What is also great is that you can turn on subtitles in Spanish or English or both at the same or you can just turn them off. Also you can read the transcripts of the videos. There are some free videos but using the website costs $9.95 per month. I would call it a good investment if this seems like a way that you like to learn.

Podclub Spanish
This is a nice podcast with interesting topics. The audio is good quality too. Free! Level: upper beginner and intermediate

Radio Ambulante
Interesting stories and interviews. These podcasts have transcripts and English translations. Free!

50 languages - Spanish
Great collection of phrases for different situations. They also have a nice smart phone app. Free!

Simply Learn Italian - Travel Phrasebook for Italy
A great mobile app (Android and iOS) that will teach you lots of useful phrases. Audio included. I love the fact that you can save your favorite phrases so you can study the sentences you need more practically. There is also a flashcard function that seemed to be of good quality. The basic version is free, but if you pay you get to study more sentences.
