How to use (and how not to use) this website

For more or less serious students of Russian: this website does not pretend to be a comprehensive language course. There are many ways to get fluent in a language, but usually you need a combination of different methods and materials. I will not, for example, teach much grammar here. There are many books and websites for that.

This website focuses more on conversation, the amazing world of human interaction.

There are many ways that you can memorize the useful sentences that this website teaches. It's probably a good idea to say them out loud. You can write them down in your notes, and you can imagine different situations where you can use them. And most imporantly, use them in real life, in your conversations and messages. Language is a skill and we learn skills by doing (ie. talking and writing to people).
