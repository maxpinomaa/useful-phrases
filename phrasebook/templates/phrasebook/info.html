About this website

This website was created by Max Pinomaa from Helsinki, Finland. You can contact me firstname.surname@gmail.com.

This website does not pretend to be a comprehensive language course. I will not, for example, teach much grammar here. There are many books and websites for that. There are many ways to get fluent in a language, but usually you need a combination of different methods and materials.

This website focuses more on conversation, the amazing world of human interaction.

There are many ways that you can memorize the useful sentences that this website teaches. It's probably a good idea to say them out loud. You can write them down in your notes, and you can imagine different situations where you could use them. And most imporantly, use them in real life, in your conversations and messages. Language is a skill and we learn skills by doing (ie. talking and writing to people).
