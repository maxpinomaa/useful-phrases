Welcome! If you want to learn Spanish, this website may be useful for you. We will cover expressions that are useful in a conversation. The main example sentences will have audio which is important for getting used to the pronounciation. Also hearing them many times will make them easier to remember.

If you don't know them yet, learning the common questions everybody asks you is a good idea. When you know them (and how to answer to them) it will be much easier to have little conversations with the native speakers. Even if your vocabulary is still relatively small!

Conversational connectors make your sentences longer, more interesting and more fluent. An example:
